## 1.0.0 – 2026-01-10

### Added

- Initial release of Birthday Widget
- Dashboard widget showing birthdays grouped by Today, Upcoming, and Recent
- Age display for contacts with birth year information
- Smart date formatting with locale-aware display (Today, Tomorrow, Yesterday, weekday names)
- Special highlighting for today's birthdays with cake emoji
- Admin settings for configurable date range (days past/future)
- Birthday calendar status detection with enable/disable functionality
